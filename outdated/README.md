# /outdated

Files moved out of the active pipeline during the 2026 revision for Socius.
Nothing here is referenced by the current scripts; keep for reference only.

## Why these were moved

- **`scholar_scraping/`** — The original Google Scholar + RSelenium scraping infrastructure (`02main.R`, `scrape_scholar_function.R`, `helper_functions.R`). Retired in favor of OpenAlex via the `openalexR` package. The Scholar approach was captcha-prone, slow, lacked author disambiguation (forcing manual workarounds for the two Collinses and Dorothy Smith), and would have been a methodological weak point against Socius editors.


- **`output_data/`** — Old Google Scholar scrapes (11 hand-picked theorists × {AJS, ASR, ST, SoE}). Superseded because (a) the theorist list expanded from 11 to 25 via the externally validated textbook procedure, and (b) the journal panel swapped Sociology of Education for Social Forces (aligning with the Du Bois revision). AJS/ASR/ST scrapes for the 11 overlapping authors are technically reusable; left here for now in case we want to selectively recover.
- **`final_data/`** — `viz_soc_theory_data.RDS`, derived from the old scrapes.
- **`input_data_derived/`** — `journal_names.RDS` and `journals_authors.RDS`, both regenerated by the new `01input_data.R`.
- **`data_viz/`** — Old figures and intermediate PNGs/SVGs. All will be regenerated.
- **`04viz.R`, `05network_viz.R`, `figure_paper.R`** — Old figure-construction scripts. Useful as reference when building the new variants in `04viz_variants.R`.
- **`lived_experience/`** — A separate side project (keyword-based scrape for "lived experience", "habitus", etc. in the same journals). Not part of the theorists paper.
- **`quick_scrape/`** — Another side project.
